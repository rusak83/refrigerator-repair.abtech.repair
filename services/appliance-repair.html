Common Issues We Fix

  • No cooling or over-freezing
  • Leaking water or drainage failures
  • Not heating, uneven temperatures
  • Strange noises, shaking, or vibration
  • Error codes and sensor faults
  • Electrical or breaker trips
  • Ice buildup or slow ice production
  • Doors not sealing properly
  • Startup failures or frequent cycling

  4. Careful cleanup and service report
