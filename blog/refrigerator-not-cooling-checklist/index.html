Refrigerator Not Cooling Checklist

Use this quick checklist to spot obvious issues before you call for service.

Quick checks

  1. Verify the temperature setting and power.
  2. Check for blocked vents or over-packed shelves.
  3. Inspect door gaskets for gaps or tears.
  4. Listen for abnormal fan or compressor noise.
  5. Look for visible ice buildup or airflow blockage.

If the fridge is still warm, professional diagnostics can prevent food loss and secondary damage.
